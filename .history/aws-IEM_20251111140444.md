# ** Infrastructure Event Management.**

### Explanation:

**AWS Infrastructure Event Management (IEM)** is a feature available under the **Enterprise Support plan** that provides **proactive architectural and scaling guidance** during:

* Product launches,
* Migrations, or
* Major events expected to cause a surge in traffic.

AWS experts work with you to prepare, monitor, and ensure your infrastructure scales smoothly.
