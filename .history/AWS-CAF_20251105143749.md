# AWS CAF
The AWS CAF is a framework that brings AWS experience and best practices to companies preparing to migrate to the AWS Cloud. The framework provides tools to help accelerate the migration journey, organize resources, and align management during the transition.

*Benefits:*
 AWS CAF provides benefits for migrations to reduce business risk and improve sustainability and corporate transparency. Companies can grow revenue by creating new products and services in their cloud transformation. They can also reduce operational costs, increase productivity, and improve customer experience in their new cloud environment.

*Use cases:*
 You can use AWS CAF to migrate technology like legacy infrastructure and applications. You can also use it to migrate and optimize business processes, operations, and even create new business models with the move to the cloud


 Definition: AWS Cloud Adoption Framework (CAF) – A guide that helps organizations prepare, plan and manage cloud migration across business, people, and technology areas.

Benefits: Reduces risk, improves sustainability, boosts revenue, lowers costs, and enhances productivity and customer experience.

Use Cases: Migrates legacy systems, optimizes operations, and builds new cloud-based business models.

| Perspective        | Focus Area                                    | Key Insight                                                    |
| :----------------- | :-------------------------------------------- | :------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **1️⃣ Business**   | Align cloud adoption with business goals      | Focuses on value realization, ROI, and business outcomes       |

| **3️⃣ Governance** | Strategy, portfolio, and risk management      | Establish accountability, compliance, and financial management |
| **4️⃣ Platform**   | Design, build, and operate cloud environments | Focuses on architecture, automation, and scalability           |
| **5️⃣ Security**   | Protect data and systems                      | Identity, compliance, and threat management                    |
| **6️⃣ Operations** | Manage and optimize workloads                 | Continuous improvement, monitoring, and incident response      |
