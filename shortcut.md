CAF - Migration journey

CAF: strategic, roadmap, organization, migration, cloud journey

WAF: technical, workloads, best practices, secure, efficient

* The **AWS Cloud Adoption Framework (CAF)** provides a **strategic, business-focused roadmap** for **migrating to the cloud**, while the

 **AWS Well-Architected Framework (WAF)** provides **technical and operational best practices** for **building and operating individual workloads** on AWS.

* **CAF** helps **align the organization** and **plan the overall strategy**, whereas 

**WAF** helps ensure **specific workloads** are **secure, reliable, efficient, and cost-effective**. 

* Think of **CAF** as the "**why** and **how**" for the entire **cloud journey**, and **WAF** as the "**how**" for building individual "**what**" **components** of that journey.
